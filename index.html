A comprehensive toolkit for photographers covering ND filters, exposure equivalents, film reciprocity, golden hour planning, and astrophotography. Designed for field work and studio use, with a special Night Mode for preserving night vision.

🔒 Fully Offline • Complete Privacy

ExposureLab works completely offline — no internet connection required for any feature, including Golden Hour calculations. Your settings, presets, and location are never stored, logged, or transmitted anywhere. No login, no account, no cloud sync.

Tools

🌅 Golden Hour Planner PRO NEW

Plan every light phase of the day — astronomical dawn, nautical dawn, blue hour, sunrise, golden hour, solar noon, and their evening counterparts — calculated on-device for your exact GPS location. Sunrise time is free; all other phase times require Pro. Typically accurate to ±1 minute.

Times are for photographic planning only. Not for navigation, aviation, or safety-critical use. Actual conditions depend on weather and terrain.

🌄 ND Filter Calculator FREE

Calculate adjusted shutter speeds for ND filter stacks. Stack multiple filters (ND2 through ND1000) and get instant results. Includes an integrated countdown timer with haptic and sound alerts. Save filter combinations as presets.

⚖️ Exposure Converter FREE

Find equivalent exposures at the same EV. Adjust aperture, shutter speed, or ISO and instantly see matching settings. Full ISO 50–102400 and ƒ/1.0–ƒ/32 range with Pro.

🎞️ Film Reciprocity Calculator FREE

Reciprocity failure corrections for 58 professional film stocks — Kodak, Ilford, Fujifilm, CineStill, Harman Phoenix, Lomography, and more. Includes an integrated countdown timer. 4 stocks free; full library with Pro.

🌌 Astrophotography Calculator FREE

Calculate maximum shutter speed to prevent star trails using the 250 Rule, 300 Rule, and the advanced Best Rule. The Best Rule accounts for your specific lens aperture and sensor pixel size. Supports 11 camera sensors including Sony, Canon, Nikon, and OM System.

Inspired by the work of Frédéric Michaud and the astrophotography community.

🎬 Film Explorer PRO

Browse all 58 film stocks with reciprocity formulas, preview corrections interactively, and discover films by category. Color, B&W, Slide, and Cinematic filters with full search.

Save filter stacks, timer settings, and astrophotography camera/lens combinations. Free users get 1 preset per tool; Pro unlocks unlimited saves across all tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ExposureLab free? All five core tools — ND Filter Calculator, Exposure Converter, Film Reciprocity, Astrophotography Calculator, and Golden Hour (sunrise time) — are free. ExposureLab Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks all phase times in Golden Hour, the full 58-film library, custom film stocks, Film Explorer, full ISO/aperture range, unlimited presets, astro presets, and educational guides.
Does ExposureLab require an internet connection? No. Every feature works completely offline, including Golden Hour solar calculations. Nothing is sent to any server.
How does Golden Hour work? ExposureLab uses the NOAA Solar Calculator algorithm to compute all light phase times on your device using only your GPS coordinates and the selected date. No internet is required. Typical accuracy is ±1 minute for latitudes between 60°S and 60°N. Times are for photographic planning only and should not be used for navigation or safety-critical purposes.
Is my location stored or shared? No. Your location is used only to perform the solar calculation on your device. It is never stored, logged, or transmitted to any server. ExposureLab has no access to your location history.
How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device? Open ExposureLab, navigate to any Pro feature, and tap "Restore Purchase." Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and restores instantly at no charge.
What film stocks are included? 58 stocks including Kodak Portra 160/400/800, Kodak Ektar 100, Kodak Tri-X 400, Kodak T-Max 100/400, Ilford HP5 Plus, Ilford Delta 100/400/3200, Ilford FP4, Fujifilm Velvia 50/100, Fujifilm Provia 100F, CineStill 800T, CineStill 400D, Harman Phoenix 200, Kodak Ektachrome E100, Ilford Ortho Plus 80, Lomography Color 200, and many more. 4 stocks are free; all 58 unlock with Pro.
What cameras does the Astrophotography Calculator support? The Best Rule supports custom pixel size entry for any camera. Presets are provided for: Sony A7R IV/V (3.76µm), OM System OM-1 II (3.44µm), Nikon Z8/Z9 (4.34µm), Nikon Z7/Z7 II (4.35µm), Canon R5 (4.39µm), Sony A7 IV (5.12µm), Nikon Z6 III (5.94µm), Canon R8 (5.98µm), Canon R6 (6.57µm), Canon R3 (7.39µm), and Sony A7S III (8.40µm).
Can I create my own film stock? Yes, with Pro. Use the Custom Film Stock feature to enter any multiplier (×) or exponent (^) reciprocity formula and save it to your library alongside the built-in stocks.
What is the ND Filter seeding — will I lose my filters on update? No. The filter database is additive: updates only add missing default filters (such as ND16 and ND32) and never remove or overwrite filters you have saved.
How do I report a bug or suggest a feature? Email bandurria.apps@gmail.com with a description and, if possible, your device model and iOS version.

Overview

  • No name, email address, or account information
  • No usage analytics or crash reports sent to us

Location

The Golden Hour Planner requests your device location to calculate solar phase times. This is performed entirely on-device using the NOAA Solar Calculator algorithm. Your location is never stored beyond the current session, never logged, and never transmitted to any server or third party. You may deny location access at any time in iOS Settings — the rest of the app is unaffected.

Presets, settings, and custom film stocks are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not backed up to any external server by ExposureLab. It may be included in your iCloud device backup if you have iCloud Backup enabled in iOS Settings.

In-App Purchases

ExposureLab Pro is purchased through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information. Purchase history is managed by your Apple ID.

Third-Party Services

ExposureLab does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The app uses Apple's CoreLocation framework for on-device location access and Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases — both governed by Apple's privacy policies.

Children's Privacy
